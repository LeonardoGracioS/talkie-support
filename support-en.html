Support — Talkie

AI Speech Assistant

Talkie is a communication aid app for people living with ALS, speech disabilities, or anyone who needs help speaking.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Talkie work?

Talkie listens to your conversation partner through the microphone, understands the context, and suggests responses for you. Tap a suggestion and Talkie speaks it aloud in a natural voice.

Is an internet connection required?

Basic text-to-speech works offline. AI-generated suggestions and voice cloning features require an internet connection.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All your data stays on your device. No backend server, no analytics, no tracking. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I clone my voice?

Go to Settings → record voice samples → Talkie creates a digital copy of your voice using ElevenLabs technology. You'll need an ElevenLabs API key.

Which languages are supported?

French, English, Spanish, Portuguese, German, Italian, Arabic, and more.
