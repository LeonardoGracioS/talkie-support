Frequently asked questions

How do I set up cloned voice?

Create an account on ElevenLabs, get your API key, then enter it in the app settings. You can then record voice samples and clone your voice.

AI suggestions don’t work

AI suggestions require iOS 26 or later with Apple Intelligence enabled. On earlier versions, use manual input (keyboard button).

How do I delete my data?

Open app settings, go to the Personal data section, then tap Delete all my data.
