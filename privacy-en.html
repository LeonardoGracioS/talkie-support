Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Talkie is a communication aid app for people living with ALS, people who are non-speaking, or who have difficulty speaking. Your privacy is our priority.

Data we collect

  • Username — stored only on your device, used to personalize suggestions.
  • Conversation transcripts — stored only on your device (last 20 exchanges maximum). Never sent to an external server.
  • Patient memory — personal notes stored locally, used to contextualize AI suggestions.
  • ElevenLabs API key — provided by you, stored securely in the iOS Keychain. Used only for ElevenLabs API calls.
  • Voice samples — stored locally. Sent to ElevenLabs only when you initiate voice cloning.

Third-party services

  • ElevenLabs (optional) — speech synthesis and voice cloning. When you enable this feature, your explicit consent is required. Text to speak and/or voice samples are sent to ElevenLabs servers. Talkie does not retain this data. See ElevenLabs privacy policy.
  • Apple Intelligence (iOS 26+) — response suggestions. Processing is done entirely on your device. No data leaves your iPhone or iPad.

What we do NOT do

  • No backend server — all data stays on your device.
  • No analytics or telemetry.
  • No advertising.
  • No cross-app tracking.
  • No sale or sharing of data with third parties.

Microphone

Talkie uses the microphone for speech recognition (Web Speech API). Audio is processed on your device and is never recorded or sent to an external server.

Deleting your data

You can delete all your data at any time from the app settings (Personal data section). This removes history, memory, voice samples, and the API key from the Keychain.

Children

Talkie is not intended for children under 13.
