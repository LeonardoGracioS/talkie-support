Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Talkie is a communication aid app for people living with ALS, speech disabilities, or anyone who needs help speaking. Your privacy is our top priority.

Data Collected

  • Username — stored only on your device, used to personalize suggestions.
  • Conversation transcripts — stored only on your device (last 20 exchanges maximum). Never sent to any external server.
  • Patient memory — personal notes stored locally, used to contextualize AI suggestions.
  • ElevenLabs API key — provided by the user, securely stored in iOS Keychain. Used only for ElevenLabs API calls.
  • Voice samples — stored locally. Sent to ElevenLabs only during voice cloning, at the user's initiative.

Third-Party Services

  • ElevenLabs (optional) — text-to-speech and voice cloning. When you enable this feature, your explicit consent is required. Text to be spoken and/or voice samples are sent to ElevenLabs servers. No data is retained by Talkie. See ElevenLabs Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Intelligence (iOS 26+) — response suggestion generation. Processing is done entirely on your device. No data leaves your iPhone/iPad.

What We Do NOT Do

  • No backend server — all data stays on your device.
  • No analytics or telemetry collection.
  • No advertisements.
  • No cross-app activity tracking.
  • No selling or sharing of data with third parties.

Microphone

Talkie uses the microphone for speech recognition (Web Speech API). Audio is processed on your device and is never recorded or sent to any external server.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from the app settings ("Personal Data" section). This action removes history, memory, voice samples, and the API key from the Keychain.

Children

Talkie is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
